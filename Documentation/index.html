Our Services

Design & Installation of LPG Systems

We are seasoned professionals in the design, installation, testing and commissioning of LPG systems in line with the Kenya Standard KS 1938-6:2012 on the handling, storage and distribution of Liquefied Petroleum Gas in domestic, commercial, and industrial installations – code of practice.

We utilize several technologies that have proved advantageous overtime, which are:

1. Flexible Gas Pipe systems

Advancements in technology have led to the use of plastic pipework systems as a cost-effective and efficient means of LPG gas reticulation for both above ground and underground applications. We specialize in Press-fit pipe systems, which are composed of a cross-linked polyethylene (PEX) inner layer, an Aluminium middle layer, and a HDPE outer layer. These LPG pipe systems offer numerous benefits such as secure connections, easy workmanship, flexibility, and faster installation time.

2. Smart Gas Leak Detection Systems

Ensuring the safety of LPG gas pipe systems through early detection and rectification of gas leaks is crucial for their safe operation. Smart LPG gas leak detection systems are a vital preventive measure that detects potential leaks in a timely manner, guaranteeing the safety of the entire gas installation and ensuring the system remains safe for use throughout its lifespan.

3. Building Information Modelling (BIM)

We utilize advanced design techniques, such as Building Information Modelling (BIM), to optimize the design and documentation process, accurately estimate project costs, and prevent material waste on site. BIM is a process that generates and manages building data throughout its lifecycle, from concept to operation and maintenance, using digital modeling tools. BIM provides several benefits to projects, such as cost savings, proper coordination, and improved collaboration among project team members, making it a key technology for LPG gas system design and documentation.

Inspection and Maintenance of LPG Systems

We offer routine inspection and maintenance services for LPG systems. Our maintenance procedures cover the following:

a) Quarterly preventive maintenance – These are quarterly site visitations that encompass will involve:

  • Site inspection and report of status of the systems and offering recommendations.
  • Supply and repair of defective equipment.
  • Carryout additional training within the maintenance period to ensure all client staff are up to date

b) Breakdown maintenance – Emergency repairs of parts that may have broken-down.

c) Health, safety, and environment.

d) Periodic LPG installation checklist.

e) Training client on Operational and maintenance of all installed systems & services

f) Services Costing.
